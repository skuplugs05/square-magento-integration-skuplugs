What is Square Magento Integration?

Square Magento integration connects your Square POS system with your Magento eCommerce store. Square is widely used for in-store transactions, inventory management, and payment processing, while Magento powers robust online stores with extensive customization capabilities.

By integrating these two platforms using SKUPlugs, businesses can synchronize data such as products, inventory, and orders in real time. This eliminates the need for manual data entry and ensures consistency across all sales channels.
